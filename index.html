My name is Isabel Cortright and I am a graphic design student at the Art Institute. I grew up in Miami and have been drawing and painting ever since I can remember. Some of my favorite artists include Salvador Dali and Jean-Michel Basquiat. In general, I enjoy all forms of art besides drawing and painting such as film, poetry, photography, etc. I'm 20 years old and hope to one day have my own gallery.
